Learn English Sign Language

Greetings - 'Hello'

The sign for "Hello" is a simple, welcoming gesture. To do this, raise your hand to your forehead and move it outward like a wave.

Gratitude - 'Thank You'

To express "Thank You," place your fingertips near your chin and move your hand away slightly, almost as if you are blowing a kiss.

Politeness - 'Sorry'

Use the sign for "Sorry" by placing your hand on your chest and moving it in small circles. This is a polite way to express regret.

Affirmation - 'Yes'

To say "Yes," you can mimic the motion of nodding your head. This is done by moving your hand up and down in a fist-like gesture.
